Origin Story

Built from
the Mic Up

Angkor Comedy Siem Reap grew directly out of Cambodia's established stand-up scene. Comedy in Cambodia (est. 2011) has been hosting shows, open mics, and workshops across the country for over 14 years. That is where Princella and Freddy found their footing.

They first crossed paths at a 2022 open mic night at Noisy Chilli, Phnom Penh. That night became the real starting point of both their comedy journeys.

After performing across the capital, they relocated to Siem Reap in 2024 and began building something new. What started as Comedy Social at venues across the city found its home at The Panthers Hut and Pomme Siem Reap, growing into a monthly showcase welcoming resident and visiting talent from across Asia and beyond.

Comedy is the hardest form of performing arts, but the most healing form of art. Laughter is the best medicine, and Siem Reap's last two years of progress have truly proved it.
Princella Anum Gill, Co-Founder

Princella Anum Gill

Co-Founder  ·  Science Teacher  ·  Restaurant Owner

Princella migrated from Pakistan and made Cambodia her family's home. She found her community at open mic nights, turning her journal observations on life in Cambodia into stand-up. She took the Comedy in Cambodia course, delivered a breakout 17-minute debut set, and has not stopped since.

Freddy Maze

Co-Founder  ·  Economics  ·  Theater

Freddy came to one open mic with friends, shared his material, and had a lightning-bolt moment: his economics and theater background was always leading him toward the stage. He has been building from that night ever since, performing, producing, and growing the Siem Reap scene.
